    IIIT Kottayam


    FOUNDATION

    IIIT Kottayam was founded in 2015. In 2017, IIITK was declared as an Institute of National Importance by IIIT PPP Bill 2017. The IIIT Kottayam's permanent campus is located at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam district, Kerala. The work for development of the permanent campus is entrusted to CPWD, GoI. The campus is designed to be environment friendly and green with the provision for solar and wind energy.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started several clubs and are learning a lot of skills. All the students actively participate in the Technical and cultural activities. Dhruva-annual Tech fest, Pradva-annual sports meet, Enlance-cultural fest are some of the events in which the students are deeply involved and developing their skills.

    NEWS & UPDATES

    ICITIIT-20

    IEEE Conference

    IIIT Kottayam organised an International Conference on Innovative Trends In Information Technology (ICITIIT-20) in the presence of Dr. Rajiv V. Dharaskar, Director,IIIT Kottayam,Prof. Dr. Michael Gerndt, TUM - Germany, Shri.M Radakrishnan, Registrar, IIIT Kottayam, Prof.Dr. M.P. Rajan, Dean Academics, IIIT Kottayam, Dr. Kumaravel S, Assistant Professor, NIT Calicut, Dr. Shajulin Benedict,Assistant Professor, IIIT Kottayam and Dr. Bakkiyaraj, Assistant Professor, IIIT Kottayam. As part of the conference invited lectures has also been delivered on the topic covering the IoT, Data Science, and Cloud topics.

    EBSB - Ek Bharat Shreshtha

    Bharat

    Under the banner of EBSB club and cultural club of IIIT Kottayam, the students of IIIT Kottayam organised different plays representing different and cultures of India showing the unity in diversity. To make it more successful and to implement the idea of EBSB in a better way, the studentswere chosen randomly to represent states of India and their traditions. Moreover, all the plays were live streamed on different social media platform to make people aware about EBSB.



    Pradva - Annual Sports

    meet

    Sportec (sports club), organised and promoted sports meet, in sports such as Athletics, Cricket, Badminton, Football, Table Tennis, Volleyball and many other indoor games. As sports must be a part of students life to teach sportsmenship, discipline, to accept failure, to learn team spirit and much more. Director, Registrar and faculties were there to encourage students. The students enjoyed the sports meet to the fullest.



    Diwali 2k19

    Diwali 27th October, 2019 was the 1st Diwali to be celebrated in our campus. Cultural programmes were organised. The celebration began by the lakshmipujan followed by many cultural events. On this day, whole was was lit with diyas. Special menu was made for the eve of Diwali by Mess committee.



    Dhruva - The Technical Fest

    Dhruva was held from the 25th to the 26th of October 2019 attempted to encompass a wide variety of events such as Debuggathon, E-Sports, Rev code, Symbiosis, Andhadhun. Dhruva was organised by Tech Club to encourage students abouts coding and other technical activites as working for the projects such as IOT. Director encouraged students more about organising such events and to make it reach on higher level by organizing inter-IIIT tech fest.
